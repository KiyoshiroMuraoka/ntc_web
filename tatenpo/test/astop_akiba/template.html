Customs Tax
International Buyers Please Note

Import duties, taxes, and charges are not included in the item price or shipping cost. These charges are the buyer's responsibility.

Please check with your country's customs office to determine what these additional costs will be prior to bidding or buying.

Customs fees are normally charged by the shipping company or collected when you pick the item up. These fees are not additional shipping charges.

Please Note

We won't under-value merchandise or mark the item as a gift on customs forms.
Doing that is against U.S. and international laws.
We only ship to PayPal registered address.
Please contact us if you reside in countries in which we do not specify shipping service and cost.
Please be aware that delivery could be delay depending on customs inspection, in case of natural disasters, and other reasons such as postal strikes.

Concerning the Prize

These are prizes from crane games like those made by SEGA and TAITO.
They are not for sale on the market and therefore have no established price.

Concerning the "Made in China" Tag

The majority of figures produced in Japan are made in China.
This simply shows the country in which the item was made. The Items we carry are all genuine products designed and manufactured by Japanese companies so you have nothing to fear.
